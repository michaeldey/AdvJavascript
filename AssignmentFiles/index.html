Why I am studying Computer Science

I have always been interested in computers and computer science. In my day to day work, I often have to write code in order to create an effect, or to get the computer to do a lot of work that I either can't, or don't want to do.
Because of this, I have noticed that I have been most excited figuring out the code behind something over actually completing a piece of animation. I have also started writing small codes for fun, or to help me solve problems about things that I think about. When I realized that this is a true passion of mine, I decided to get some formal training.
And that's why I'm in this class as well as why I'm studying at SLCC.
